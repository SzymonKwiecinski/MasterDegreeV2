\textbf{Linear Programming Model:}

\[
\begin{aligned}
    & \text{Maximize} && Z = \sum_{j=1}^{M} \text{price}_j \times x_j \\
    & \text{subject to} \\
    & && \sum_{j=1}^{M} \text{req}_{i,j} \times x_j \leq \text{available}_i, \quad i = 1, 2, \ldots, N \\
    & && x_j \geq 0, \quad j = 1, 2, \ldots, M \\
\end{aligned}
\]
