\textbf{Objective:}

Maximize the total revenue:

\[
\text{Maximize} \quad Z = \sum_{j=1}^{M} \text{price}_j \cdot x_j
\]

\textbf{Subject to:}

\begin{align*}
\sum_{j=1}^{M} \text{req}_{i,j} \cdot x_j & \leq \text{available}_i, \quad \forall i = 1, 2, \ldots, N \\
x_j & \geq 0, \quad \forall j = 1, 2, \ldots, M
\end{align*}
