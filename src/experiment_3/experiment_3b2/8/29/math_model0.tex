\textbf{Objective:}

Minimize \( z \)

\textbf{Subject to:}

\[
y_k - (a + bx_k) \leq z, \quad \text{for } k = 1, \ldots, K
\]

\[
-(y_k - (a + bx_k)) \leq z, \quad \text{for } k = 1, \ldots, K
\]

Variables:

- \( a \): intercept
- \( b \): slope
- \( z \): maximum absolute deviation, to be minimized
